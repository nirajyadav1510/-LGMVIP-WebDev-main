Historic Moments

the Prudential Cup '83

The 1983 Cricket World Cup (officially the Prudential Cup '83) was the 3rd edition of the Cricket World Cup tournament. It was held from 9 to 25 June 1983 in England and Wales and was won by India. Eight countries participated in the event. The 1983 World Cup was full of dramatic cricket all through the tournament.

2007 ICC World Twenty20

The 2007 ICC World Twenty20 was the inaugural T20 Int. cricket world championship, Hosted by South Africa from 11-24 September 2007.12 teams took part in the thirteen-day tournament-the ten Test-playing nations and the finalists of the 2007 WCL Division One tournament.India won the tournament.

2011 ICC Cricket World Cup

The 2011 ICC Cricket World Cup was the tenth Cricket World Cup. It was played in India, Sri Lanka, and (for the first time) Bangladesh. India won the tournament, defeating Sri Lanka by 6 wickets in the final at Wankhede Stadium in Mumbai, thus becoming the first country to win the Cricket World Cup final on home soil.

2013 ICC Champions Trophy

The 2013 ICC Champions Trophy was the 17th ICC Champions Trophy, a ODI cricket tournament held in England and Wales between 6-23 June 2013.3 cities hosted the tournament's matches: London , Birmingham and Cardiff.India won the competition, beating England by five runs in the final after overcoming South Africa

Legends of Indian Cricket

Sunil Manohar Gavaskar

Sunil Gavaskar regarded as one of the greatest batsmen and one of the best opening batsmen in Test cricket history

Kapil Dev Ramlal Nikhanj

He was a fast medium bowler and a hard hitting middle order batsman.

Mithali Dorai Raj

She is the highest run-scorer in women's international cricket and the only female cricketer to surpass the 7,000 run mark in Women's One Day International matches

Jhulan Nishit Goswami

An all-rounder who bats right-handed and bowls right-arm medium fast

Rahul Sharad Dravid

Rahul Dravid is a former Indian cricketer and former captain of the Indian national team. He is the Director of Cricket Operations at National Cricket Academy, Bengaluru.

Mahendra Singh Dhoni

Under his captaincy,India won the inaugural 2007 ICC World Twenty20, the 2010 and 2016 Asia Cups, the 2011 ICC Cricket World Cup and the 2013 ICC Champions Trophy.
